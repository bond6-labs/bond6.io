Before you write

Bond6.io is not a platform for content. It is a terminal for transmission. Before you submit, ask yourself one question: did writing this cost me something?

If the answer is yes — if there was a moment where you almost stopped, where the sentence became too honest, where you had to look away from the screen — then you are in the right place.

✓

We welcome

Essays, fragments, dispatches, letters, reflections, confessions, observations, and anything that doesn't fit neatly into a category.

✓

We welcome imperfection

The typo born from trembling hands. The sentence that trails off. The paragraph that contradicts the one before it. Leave them in.

✗

We do not accept

AI-generated text, SEO content, promotional material, or anything written without a pulse. We can tell the difference.

✗

We do not optimize

We will not ask you to shorten your sentences, add subheadings, or make it more "readable." We will only ask: is this true?
